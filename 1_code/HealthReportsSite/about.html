Leaner analytics for a leaner you

The goal of this site and application is to help streamline the process of finding out more about the current health trends of yourself and those around you.

Not only will you have the ability to enter in your workout information, you'll be able to compare it to those around to compare and contrast different metrics pertaining to health.

Some include:

  • Run time & Distance
  • Averaging splits
  • Caloric Intake

The Healthy Community

Not only will our project allow you to have a consolidated place for all of your health information, you'll also be able to look at health trends from your community and those around you. Has everyone been slowly improving their running times over 5 miles? Is the average bike ride aorund 12 miles?
