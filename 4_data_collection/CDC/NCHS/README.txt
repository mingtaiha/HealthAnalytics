CDC's National Center of Health Statistics:

This folder contains the data collected/extracted from the CDC's National Center of Health Statistics.
This data was extracted manually as the data is present in a PDF and CSV file.

Link to PDF file: http://www.cdc.gov/nchs/data/nhsr/nhsr010.pdf

This data was utilized for stratified montecarlo sampling to generate our test data.

Coulumns:
ethnicity, gender, age group, examined count, mean, standard error, 5, 10, 15, 25, 50, 75, 85, 90, 95
*note:* the last 9 columns are the percentiles, from 5th to 95th.

Files:
"nchs_weights.csv" -- Contains weight distributions (in kg) by ethnicity, gender and age.
"nchs_heights.csv" -- Contains height distributions (in cm) by ethnicity, gender and age.
"nchs_waist.csv" -- Contains waist circumference distributions (in cm) by ethnicity, gender and age.
